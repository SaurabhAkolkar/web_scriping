ANNUAL REPORT 2024-2025
The annual report of our institution namely Shri Trimbakraj Bahuuddeshiya Sevabhavi Sanstha
Galli No.5, New Hanuman Nagar, Aurangabad is mentioned as below:-
Senior Citizens Home for 50 elderly women :-

This unit is running from four years. Above 65 years aged Senior Citizen Persons had given admission. The admission had given 50 beneficials and they are taken benefit. Different facilities are given by this institution to the beneficials such as entertainment, medical treatment, free lodging and boarding for Senior Citizen Persons, paper reading, magazines and newspapers, Colour T.V. are available for entertainment to them, short programs and cultural program are arranged by institution and clothes are given to all beneficials. There is taken exercise of beneficials i.e. Yoga, Pranayam etc. All this facilities are given free by this institution.
Hostel for 100 students for S.C. category :-

In this hostel is started by the institution since last three years. Hundred scheduled caste students have been given admission.There are total 100 students admitted in both Secondary and Higher Secondary education. The students have been given facilities are given below :- Breakfast, midday meal, dinner and residential arrangement. Colouur T.V. for entertainment and news papar facilities are given for to them. Medical health checking facility also given to them time to time. Uniforms, books and stationery also distributed to each and every students.
For all round development of students, the facilities of exercise, games, music are given to them by their choice.Only Poor and needy scheduled caste students are given admission in hostel. All above facilities are given free of cost by the Institution to students.
President/Founder = P.B. Darunte

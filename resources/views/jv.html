ANNUAL REPORT 2024-2025
Various Projects are running through the institution “Jivan Vikas Pratisthan” Aurangabad.
Senior Citizen Home :-

This unit is running from four years. Above 65 years old persons had given admission in this institution. 25 beneficiaries are taking benefit. Different facilities given to beneficiaries, such as entertainment, medical treatment, free lodging and boarding for Senior Citizens, paper reading, short programmes etc. Magazines and news papers are available for reading to them. There is taken exercise of beneficieries i.e. Yoga, Pranayam etc. All this facilities are given free by this institution.
Mobile Medicare Unit for Senior Citizen :-

This project is running from two years, mobile Medicare Unit for Senior Citizen. The poor and needy people have taken benefit of it. Peoples who are in a very bad condition have got benefit of this Scheme. So, this Scheme has received prestige for us. In this scheme, the medicines and treatment have been given free to them. The number of visits 225 conducted annually.
